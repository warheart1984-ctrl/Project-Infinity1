# The Continuity Field Equation

**Status:** Normative (conceptual dynamics)  
**Derived from:** Lagrangian of Continuity

---

## Lagrangian

\[
L_{\text{cont}} = T_{\text{judgment}} - V_{\text{constraint}}
\]

Where:

- \(T_{\text{judgment}}\) = calibration velocity (rate of correction)
- \(V_{\text{constraint}}\) = invariant drift, insulation, ossification

---

## Euler–Lagrange form

\[
\frac{d}{dt}\left(\frac{\partial L_{\text{cont}}}{\partial \dot{J}}\right) - \frac{\partial L_{\text{cont}}}{\partial J} = F_{\text{reality}}
\]

Expanding:

\[
\frac{d}{dt}(C \cdot \dot{J}) + \nabla V_{\text{constraint}}(J) = F_{\text{reality}}
\]

Where:

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| \(J(t)\) | Judgment state |
| \(\dot{J}(t)\) | Correction rate |
| \(C\) | Corrigibility coefficient |
| \(F_{\text{reality}}\) | Contradiction force from evidence |

---

## Interpretation

- **Reality pushes** via contradiction (\(F_{\text{reality}}\)).
- **Invariants constrain** via drift gradients (\(\nabla V_{\text{constraint}}\)).
- **Corrigibility** determines how judgment moves (\(C\)).

Continuity holds when:

\[
C(t) > 0 \quad \text{and} \quad F_{\text{reality}} \not\to 0
\]

This is the **physics of continuity** — the dynamical account underlying K-∞.
