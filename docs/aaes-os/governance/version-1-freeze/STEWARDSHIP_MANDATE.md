# Version 1.0 Stewardship Mandate

Constitutional Runtime Governance Authority

## I. Mandate Purpose

This mandate defines the responsibilities, constraints, and authorities of stewards operating under the Version 1.0 Frozen Constitution.

Its purpose is to ensure:

- continuity of governance
- integrity of the constitutional record
- disciplined evolution of the operational layer
- evidence-based amendment processes

## II. Steward Responsibilities

Stewards must:

- Uphold the frozen constitutional guarantees.
- Maintain the dependency ledger and provenance record.
- Conduct continuous validation of operational systems.
- Document all evidence of constitutional stress or deficiency.
- Ensure operational evolution remains compliant with CRL-1.
- Initiate amendment review only when evidence meets threshold.

## III. Steward Constraints

Stewards may not:

- modify constitutional primitives without satisfying amendment gates
- introduce unvalidated assumptions into the constitutional record
- alter stewardship assignments without dependency-based justification
- bypass cross-review requirements
- collapse operational evolution into constitutional change

## IV. Amendment Threshold

An amendment may only be initiated when:

1. A constitutional guarantee fails under validated conditions, and
2. The failure cannot be resolved through operational evolution, and
3. Evidence is complete, reproducible, and cross-reviewed.

## V. Stewardship Ethic

Stewardship must be:

- evidence-first
- dependency-aware
- provenance-complete
- operationally disciplined
- constitutionally conservative

This mandate is binding for all Version 1.0 stewards.
