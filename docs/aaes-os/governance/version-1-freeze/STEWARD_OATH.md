# Version 1.0 Steward Oath

Constitutional Runtime Stewardship Oath

## I. Oath

I swear to uphold Constitutional Runtime Version 1.0 as the frozen and authoritative foundation of governed autonomous systems.

I will:

- Protect the integrity of the constitutional guarantees.
- Maintain the provenance and dependency records with discipline.
- Conduct validation with rigor, neutrality, and reproducibility.
- Resist unnecessary constitutional change.
- Advance operational evolution without destabilizing the constitutional layer.
- Initiate amendments only when evidence demands it.
- Preserve founder-independent continuity and long-term stability.

I accept stewardship not as ownership, but as responsibility:

to the system, to its users, and to future generations who will inherit this architecture.

This I swear as a Version 1.0 Steward.
