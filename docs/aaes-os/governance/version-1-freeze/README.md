## Operating Doctrine

Version 1.0 adopts **Validation Before Amendment**:

1. Validation is the proving ground.
2. Operational evolution is the engine of progress.
3. Constitutional evolution is rare, evidence-backed, and procedurally gated.

The constitutional layer is frozen. Operational systems, execution engines, governance workflows, and evolutionary modules may continue to advance as long as they do not modify Tier-1 primitives or violate CRL-1 guarantees.
