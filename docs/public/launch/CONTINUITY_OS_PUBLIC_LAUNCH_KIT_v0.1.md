### E. Public FAQ

**What is continuity?**  
The measurable propagation of corrigibility across stewards and generations.

**Why is preservation not enough?**  
A log of past calibrations does not guarantee future stewards become more calibrated.

**What does propagation mean?**  
A new steward improves judgment (ΔA ≥ τA) after replaying lineage alone.

**How does the system prevent tampering?**  
Hash-chained receipts, proof bundles, multi-stage governance validation, and red-team fixtures.
