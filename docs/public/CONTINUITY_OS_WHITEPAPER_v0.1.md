## Abstract

Continuity OS is a constitutional architecture designed to ensure that corrigibility — the ability to detect, correct, and learn from errors — propagates across stewards, models, and generations. It integrates governance, lineage, calibration, and runtime invariants into a unified system capable of preserving and regenerating learning over time.

## 1. Introduction

Modern AI systems lack mechanisms for preserving and transferring corrigibility. Continuity OS addresses this by defining:

- Constitutional invariants
- Runtime behavior
- Calibration preservation
- Lineage reconstruction
- Continuity propagation

The system is model-agnostic and future-proof.
