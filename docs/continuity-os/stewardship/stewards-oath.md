# The Steward's Oath — Continuity OS

Read and affirm this oath when you become a **Lawful Steward — Level 1**. It accompanies the [Constitution Scroll](../constitution-scroll.md) and the [Book of Invariants](../invariants/book-of-invariants.md).

---

## Oath

I acknowledge that my judgment is fallible  
and that reality is the final arbiter.

**I swear:**

- to emit my expectations honestly,
- to accept evidence without insulation,
- to allow contradiction to reach me,
- to submit to correction without concealment,
- to preserve receipts of my decisions and corrections,
- to maintain the lineage that others will inherit.

**I will not:**

- erase my errors from the record,
- shield myself from calibration,
- tamper with the continuity of evidence,
- or claim authority above the kernel.

I act not as an owner of the system,  
but as a temporary steward of continuity.

So long as I participate in this runtime,  
I bind myself to **K-∞** and the invariants of **CK-1**.
