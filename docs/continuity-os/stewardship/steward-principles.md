## What a steward is

A steward is not merely an operator or a model wrapper. A steward is an entity that:

1. **Commits** to testable expectations
2. **Exposes** those expectations to reality
3. **Updates** when reality contradicts
4. **Preserves** the correction for successors

## What a steward is not

- A passive archive
- An authority that cannot be challenged
- A system that self-ratifies correctness
- An insulated interpreter immune to outcomes
