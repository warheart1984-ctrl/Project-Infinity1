# Overview

Continuity OS is a constitutional operating system for **corrigibility** — the guarantee that reality can always recalibrate the judgment of future stewards.

## What continuity is

Continuity is **not**:

- preserved knowledge
- preserved institutions
- preserved archives
- preserved consensus

Continuity **is** the persistence of **corrigibility** across generations.

A lineage remains continuous only while reality retains the power to contradict, surprise, and correct its future stewards.

## Three layers

| Layer | Role | Key artifacts |
|-------|------|---------------|
| **Reasoning** | How judgment interprets evidence | GRR-1, decisions |
| **Calibration** | How reality changes judgment | CE-1, CRR-1 |
| **Stewardship** | How calibration transmits forward | CLG-1, handoff |

## Prime directive (K-∞)

> The system must preserve reality's ability to recalibrate the judgment of future stewards.

Everything else — evidence systems, governance engines, receipts, lineage graphs — exists to protect this single capability.

## What you can build

- **Lawful models** — any LLM or agent wrapped in `LawfulLLMAdapter`
- **Governed runtimes** — CRK-1 constitutional kernel
- **Reconstructible lineage** — CLG-1 across stewards and generations
- **Certified stewards** — operators who understand and uphold invariants
